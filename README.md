# SAP S/4HANA Fiori Case Studies

A collection of hands-on SAP S/4HANA case studies completed using SAP Fiori Launchpad. This repository demonstrates practical execution of core business processes across SAP Materials Management (MM), Sales & Distribution (SD), and Production Planning (PP).

The focus of this repository is on understanding how SAP Fiori applications are used to perform day-to-day enterprise operations rather than SAP system configuration.

---

## Modules Covered

- SAP Materials Management (MM)
- SAP Sales & Distribution (SD)
- SAP Production Planning (PP)

---

## Case Studies

### 📦 Materials Management (MM)

This case study demonstrates the complete procurement lifecycle from material creation to vendor payment.

**Topics Covered**

- Material Master
- Vendor Management
- Purchase Requisition
- Request for Quotation (RFQ)
- Supplier Quotation
- Purchase Order
- Goods Receipt
- Supplier Invoice
- Vendor Payment
- Inventory Monitoring

---

### 🛒 Sales & Distribution (SD)

This case study demonstrates the Order-to-Cash business process using SAP Fiori applications.

**Topics Covered**

- Customer Master
- Contact Person
- Sales Inquiry
- Sales Quotation
- Sales Order
- Outbound Delivery
- Billing
- Financial Document Flow

---

### 🏭 Production Planning (PP)

This case study focuses on manufacturing planning and execution within SAP S/4HANA.

**Topics Covered**

- Material Master
- Routing
- Product Groups
- Demand Management
- Material Requirements Planning (MRP)
- Planned Orders
- Production Orders
- Goods Issue
- Production Confirmation
- Goods Receipt
